# AlmaLinux Wiki

# About

AlmaLinux (opens new window) is an Open Source and forever-free enterprise Linux distribution, governed and driven by the community, focused on long-term stability and a robust production grade platform. AlmaLinux OS is 1:1 binary comptible with RHEL®. The AlmaLinux OS Foundation was established as a 501(c)(6) non-profit to steward ownership and governance of the project and it's founding grant was provided by the team behind CloudLinux OS (opens new window) and several other sponsors (listed on the website) have joined this effort as well.
